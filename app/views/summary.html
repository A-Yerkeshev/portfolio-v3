What i can do for you

  1. Build responsive webpages;

    I can build webpages with layout, that can resize and behave differently depending on which device the customer is using. This assures, that your company is not loosing a sale just because the customer have used a different device and found interface too unfriendly to use.

  2. Collect and analyze customer data;

    I can track users' activity on the page - what product is the most searched? How did customer find out about your website? How much time they spend browsing different categories of products? This data can significantly improve the efficiency of your marketing.

  3. Add registration and authentication;

    I can let users create an account on your website and safely store data. They also can log in with their Google or Facebook account. This means i can make your website require authentification to access some of its parts. Your customers can save products they like or leave reviews; your employees can take orders and serve customers digitally; your manager can monitor activity of customers and decide which products to display on front page.

  4. Fetch information from external sources;

    I can retrieve the data from third-party sources, like Wikipedia, NASDAQ or Google Maps. Some services, like weather forecasting absolutely need it. Other services can provide better customer experience with the help of the external data.

  5. Let customers share your products/services and place orders;

    Your customers will be able to share links to your products or services through social media with their friends. This helps to impove your marketing and reach larger target market without any efforts from your side. Also, i can let your customers place orders for your products right from your website, without need of calling or emailing you.

  6. Customize layout based on users' preferences;

    I can personalize the website layout based on customers' activity. I can display most relevant products on the home page; show customer relevant news and offers; allow him to modify the interface according to own needs. This contributes to long-lasting, personified relationships with the customer.

  7. Protect website from XSS attacks;

    XSS is a common way of hacking a website, when hacker includes malicious script into your webpage. By doing that, the hacker can gain access to sensitive data about your customers and your company, can make purchases in internet on your behalf. I can add Front-end layer of protection to your website, to ensure that malicious script is not executed or sent to the server

  8. Let customer upload and download files;

    I can let your customers upload their own files to your server, be it images, video, documents or whatever type else. I can let them download files that you want to share. This might be your price list, catalogue, guidebook - anything that helps you to communicate better with your customer.

  9. Add support for older browsers;

    I can ensure, that your website is properly displayed and working, even if the customer is using an outdated version of browser. Also, i can test your website for cross-browser compatibility. This helps your product to reach larger audience and not lose a sale just because the client did not update his browser.

*Please note, that possibility of implementing all of above listed services depends on the current state of your website, on which platform it was built. Everything of above listed is implementable, given that the website will be constructed from scratch.
